# The Ecom Bot

This document provides information about **The Ecom Bot**, a Discord bot designed to enhance community engagement through a comprehensive economy and activity tracking system. This bot operates within the "Empire of Shadows" server, rewarding members for their active participation.

## About The Project

The Ecom Bot focuses on creating an interactive and rewarding experience for members of the "Empire of Shadows" Discord server. Its core functionality revolves around a robust economy, leveling, and achievement system that recognizes and incentivizes user activity across various Discord interactions.

-   **Website:** https://empireofshadows.club (for general server information)
-   **Discord Invite:** https://discord.gg/ddKPwsdzjG (to join the server and experience the bot)

## Features

-   **Economy & Leveling System:**
    -   Earn Experience Points (XP) and level up through active participation (messaging, voice activity, reactions).
    -   Compete on leaderboards and earn rewards as you progress.
-   **Achievement System:**
    -   Unlock a variety of achievements by reaching specific milestones and engaging with the server.
    -   Discover unique challenges and collect rare badges.
-   **Activity Tracking:**
    -   Automatically tracks your messages, voice channel presence, and reactions.
    -   Your activity directly contributes to your leveling progress and achievement unlocks.
-   **User Settings:**
    -   **/settings opt-out**: Manage your privacy by opting out of the economy and activity tracking system. You can choose to retain your data for 90 days or have it immediately deleted.
    -   **/settings opt-in**: Rejoin the economy system at any time if you previously opted out.

## Usage

Interact with The Ecom Bot by simply participating in the server! Your messages, voice activity, and reactions will automatically contribute to your progress.

Here are some key commands related to The Ecom Bot:

-   **/settings opt-out**: Opt out of the economy and activity tracking.
-   **/settings opt-in**: Opt back into the economy and activity tracking.

Administrators have access to powerful commands for managing the bot's data for users and the guild (e.g., `/reset`, `/nuke`).
